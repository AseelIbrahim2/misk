Leaders in the Making

Teaching young people what it means to be a leader is a community responsibility, not simply a subject on a timetable.

Leadership is a set of behaviours and a way of thinking and young people can learn when they are surrounded by positive role models, and actively mentored to recognize, interpret, and emulate what they observe. These are the skills and attributes we foster.
